ABOUT THE FEST

FreedomFest started as a way for a big group of friends to come back together after being apart during lockdown — and it was such a huge success that it's now a bi-annual tradition.

Run by friends, for friends (and their friends), it's grown every single time — over 50 of us at the last one. Think long sunny afternoons by the fire-pit bus, karaoke under the tinsel, headphone discos where everyone's a DJ, wood-fired pizza, morning yoga, sound baths, and kids and dogs running riot in the best possible way.

It's not a public festival — there are no tickets on sale, no strangers, no queues. Just a private slice of Norfolk countryside, a stage, a sound system, and the people you love.

🚗 By Car

Wendling sits just off the A47 between Dereham and Swaffham — roughly 30 minutes from Norwich and easy to reach from King's Lynn. Pop NR19 2LT into your sat nav and follow signs for Wendling. There's parking on site.

🚆 By Train

The nearest mainline station is Norwich (London Liverpool Street ~1h50). From there it's about a 30-minute drive — team up in the group chat for lifts and car shares!
